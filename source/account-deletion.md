If you would like to delete your account and membership information related to this app, please follow the steps below:
1. Send an email from the Google account you used to sign in, to xialongonly@gmail.com Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - [PodsLink]"
2. We will verify your identity and delete your account and membership information within 7 business days.

## Data Deletion Scope
- Data that will be deleted: Account information, membership information
- No other personal data is stored
- Data that may be retained: Payment records may be retained for up to 180 days to comply with legal requirements
